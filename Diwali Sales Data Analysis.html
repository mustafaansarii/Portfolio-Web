Diwali Sales Data Analysis

Overview:

In this project, I conducted an exploratory data analysis (EDA) on Diwali sales data to derive insights into customer behavior and preferences. By analyzing various demographic and sales-related factors, I aimed to uncover patterns and trends that could help businesses optimize their marketing strategies and product offerings.

Data Source:

The dataset used for this analysis comprises Diwali sales data, including customer demographics, product details, and purchase information. It was sourced from [provide details of the data source].

Key Steps:

  1. Data Cleaning and Preprocessing:
    • Handled missing values by dropping rows with null values in the 'Amount' column.
    • Converted the 'Amount' column to integer type for analysis.
  2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):
    • Visualized the distribution of various demographic factors such as gender, age group, and marital status.
    • Analyzed sales trends across different states, occupation types, and product categories.
    • Identified top-selling products and customer segments contributing significantly to sales.
  3. Insights and Conclusions:
    • Married women aged 26-35 years from states like Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka, engaged in occupations such as IT, Healthcare, and Aviation, emerged as key customers driving sales.
    • Certain product categories, such as Food, Clothing, and Electronics, witnessed higher demand during the Diwali period.
    • Geographic and demographic insights can help businesses tailor their marketing campaigns and product offerings to target specific customer segments effectively.

Technologies Used:

  • Python (Pandas, NumPy) for data manipulation and analysis.
  • Matplotlib and Seaborn for data visualization.
